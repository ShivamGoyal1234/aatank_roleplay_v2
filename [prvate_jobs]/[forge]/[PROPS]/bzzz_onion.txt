This prop (bzzz_onion) must be installed from https://bzzz.tebex.io/package/5887685

Installation steps:
1. Download the prop from the Tebex store
2. Extract the files to your server resources folder
3. Add "ensure bzzz_onion" to your server.cfg
4. Restart your server

This prop is required for the Farming job in the Forge Jobs resource.
